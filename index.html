Popular Tourist Destinations

In Sri Lanka, there are hundreds, if not thousands, of tourist destinations that are so popular among tourists worldwide. Here's a little bit about some of these places.

  • Sigiriya

  • Anuradhapura

  • Nuwara Eliya

  • Arugambay

Sigiriya

Sigiriya is a palace that is built on top of a giant rock in the 5th century. It is an isolated hill. So, from the top of it, all the area around can be seen clearly.

  • It is known as the 8th wonder of the world. Because of its miraculous engineering.
  • Sigiriya has some water ponds and fountains that still work today but build around 1500 years ago.
  • According to books written in Sigiriya era, it was built by King Kashyapa. But some believe that it was a castle even before that time. They say that it was a palace that use by King Ravana 5000 years ago.

Anuradhapura

Anuradhapura is the first city ever built in Sri Lanka. The written history of Sri Lanka is starting with the Anuradhapura kingdom.

  • Anuradhapura was the capital city of Sri Lanka for one and half millennial.
  • It is one of the oldest kingdoms in the planet. Because of its long reign as a kingdom, it is known as one of the longest survived kingdoms in human civilization. (From 437 BC to 1017 AD)
  • Anuradhapura has thousands of very old tanks (man-made lakes for agriculture and water gathering). Most of those are more than 2000 years old but still used as the main water source for the people live in those areas.

Nuwara Eliya

Nuwara Eliya is one of the coldest places in Sri Lanka. Located in the beautiful mountains of Central Province. Some of the most scenic views of Sri Lanka are available here.

  • Nuwara Eliya is known as the little England.
  • Reason being the weather and climate condition is so similar to England. Sometime mist can be seen even in the middle of the day.
  • Nuwara Eliya has some of the best hotels in the country and a lot of tea plantations. Average temperature of here is around 12°C to 19°C. But in some cold night it can be dropped down to less than 5°C

Arugam Bay

Arugam Bay is one of the east coast beaches. And there are so many similar beaches in the east coast of Sri Lanka. But among foreigner Arugam Bay is so popular than other beaches.

  • Arugam Bay has perfect sea waves for surfing.
  • So many international surfing tournaments are held at Arugam Bay due to this perfect Sea condition for surfing.
  • White sandy beaches and light blue color oceans in here will give you the feel of a paradise. The beaches are so long and wide. And very good for sunbathing and resting.
