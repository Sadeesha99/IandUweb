Our Story

I and U Tours was started to give travelling enthusiast a chance to enjoy each and every part of Sri Lanka. From the beginning we were not an ordinary travelling companion. As I and U Tours We always wanted to push our limits and extend our boundaries to provide our clients with best service possible.

  • We do have a team that dedicated to please our clients.
  • Clients choice and preference will always be our priority.
  • We always want our clients to get best out of their tours.

Our company was started only few years ago. But our expertise and experiences built up in this short time is far superior to most of other. So, your tour will be on safe hands. Come and join with us to explore Sri Lanka.

Message from the Founder

"I have been fond of traveling since my childhood and during my earlier service in the Air Force I had the opportunity to travel around the country. When I was serving as an Air Force officer, I saw that the foreign tourist misses a lot of Sri Lanka. Every person should experience. So, I decided to start this organization. To give them the proper experience of visiting Sri Lanka. We have done it for the past few years and will continue to do it."
